Introduction

            SEED Infotech has initiated a series of programs which will synergize the awareness and sensitivity of citizens towards environmental issues including those that are specific to the city of Pune. SEED Infotech has adopted a school in Nande village, Taluka Mulshi. As part of our support, we have donated 1,000 books to their library, created all the physical infrastructure required for a good library, provided technical support in making it fully functional, and donated benches with writing tops to the classrooms.
